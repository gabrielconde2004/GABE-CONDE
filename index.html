A PICTURE OF ME!



My three hobbies!



-music



-dance



-outdoor leisure






One of my hobbies is music.
I have an immense passion for music.
I play guitar, drums, clarinet, and piano.
I love creating and making sound that people can enjoy.
Music has been quite an amazing journey for me.
I had to overcome obstacles that challenged me.
I would stay up learning guitar chords and melodies.
Little did I know that after four years of playing I would finally be able to read and interpret music.
I can learn and play any song with just the look of a music sheet.






Another one of my hobbies is performing cultural dance.
Performing these dances allow me to tap into my culture.
Along with it culture is something that makes me who I am.



Lastly is spending time outdoors.
I love getting a well needed break from being inside all day.
I get to feel the fresh air up against my face and feel at peace. 
Not only that, but I love to explore the beauty that Mother Nature has gifted us with.
From mountainsides to tropical beaches.
